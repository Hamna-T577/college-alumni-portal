The National Institute of Technology takes pride in its accomplished alumni who have made significant contributions in various fields. The institute recognizes the invaluable role played by its alumni in its progress, development, and future direction. The National Institute of Technology Alumni Association is a non-profit organization founded to connect, engage and support the institute's alumni community. The association was established in 2014 and is registered under the Societies Registration Act, 1860. We are pleased to share that our institute is commemorating the historic occasion of its centenary year in 2024. We eagerly anticipate meaningful and inclusive engagement with our alumni that will benefit our institution, its current students, and the broader community. We invite you to join the Alumni Association. Please feel free to reach out to us.
